About Informal Settlement Mapping and Visualization

This WebGIS platform supports spatial analysis and visualization of informal settlements in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania, covering the period 2005 to 2026. The system integrates satellite-derived indices, settlement polygons, and analytics dashboards to help planners, researchers, and communities understand growth trends and risk patterns.

The project is developed by LSM2 Group 11 at Ardhi University, Makongo, Dar es Salaam, in collaboration with applied geospatial intelligence initiatives in East Africa.

Our mission is to make informal settlement data accessible, transparent, and actionable for evidence-based urban planning and service delivery.

Use the Maps page for interactive exploration, Statistics for ward and district trends, and the User Guide for step-by-step instructions in English and Kiswahili.
