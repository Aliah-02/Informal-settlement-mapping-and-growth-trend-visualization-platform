District growth

Kinondoni has experienced rapid population growth and urbanization over the years. Many informal settlements developed in different parts of the district, leading to challenges in infrastructure, land use, and service delivery. Today, Kinondoni remains one of the areas with significant informal settlement expansion in Dar es Salaam.
